How to Play:

  • Move left: LEFT
  • Move right: RIGHT
  • Rotation: UP or <, >
  • Fall faster: DOWN
  • Drop: SPACE
  • Use Special Item: X

V2 Updated Features

  • New design for main game scene
  • S-point is now SD-point, which is calculated based on S-point (falling speed, height, and immediate drop counts) depends on how users play the game. It will increase fast or slow.
  • Level is now automatically increased after user hit a certain checkpoint score (7 levels with dif. falling speed)
  • Special items added: Users need to charge the SPoints to meet the required SPoint of the special items, item will be ready to user when users gain enough s-points. If he does not use the special item, it will expires after 15s and a new one will show up.
  • 4 Types of special items with dif. period of effects: Destroy, Reverse, Fast Fall
  • Destroy multiple lines at the same time will gain more score than single one. 4 will be gain most points
  • Timer added
