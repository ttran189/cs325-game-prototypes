Troll vs Drones

  • Originality: It's easy to play and fun.
  • Technical Merit: Nothing special.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I leaned how to to set up a project with Phaser.
    The hardest part is the concept of the game.
    The easiest part is all game physics are handled by Phaser framework and it saved a lot of development time.
  • Prototype Assets:
    • Troll face: Me Me
    • Drone: Freepik
    • Background: Freepik
    • Sound Effects: Free Sound ORG
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: - Inspired by Flappy Bird.
    - Small part of code was derived from Lessmilk Tutorial
  • High Concept: Character is a troll face that tried to avoid the drones flying to toward him. The gravity will bring him down and the players have to bring him up by a click, a tap or a space key push.
  • Theme: Tropical Forest.
  • Mandated Variety: For every second, a set of 3 drones are generated with random y values.
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is good enough for playing. It pointed out some errors and constraints that need to be improved for the final release.
  • Player Experience Goals: I hope people to see it fun and can relax with it.
  • Gameplay: N/A.
  • Strategies: Tap or click on time and be patient to go further in the game.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: No story behind.
  • Target Audience: This game targets people from 12 - 60.
  • Play Time: It depends on players. Should be 1 minute - 15 minutes.
